Our Course

Courses We Offer

These three courses we offer are listed here.

Intermediate

At the Intermediate level, we provide three different streams: Science, Management, and Law. These are taught at our Mitrapark block. We have well-educated and experienced faculty members in all three streams who support students in achieving their future goals.

Degree

In the Bachelor's level, we offer three different courses: BIT, BCS, and BBA. We focus on a practical approach to study, which is why we have created different learning models in all three courses. We offer two lecture classes, two tutorial classes, and two workshop classes per week. Additionally, we provide professional courses each semester to make students job-ready for the future.

Master's

Our Master's programs include MBA, MIT, and MCS, which are very flexible. You can attend these courses at three different times: 6-9 AM, 12-3 PM, and 5-8 PM. This allows you to join the course while continuing your job. You can even get field visits every week and hands-on practice while learning. Upon completion of this degree, you receive a degree from Lincoln University, which is highly valuable worldwide.
